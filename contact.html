FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive.

Is the National Parks Portal free to use?
Yes! The National Parks Portal is completely free. Our mission is to make park and wildlife information accessible to everyone around the world, so there is no registration or fee required to browse any section of the site.
How do I find a park near my location?
Visit the Parks List page and use the continent filter to narrow down parks by region. Each park card includes its exact location, and our interactive Google Map section shows all featured parks plotted on a global map.
Can I submit photos to the gallery?
Absolutely! We love featuring community photography. Send your high-resolution park and wildlife photos to info@nationalparks.com with the subject line "Gallery Submission." Include the park name, location, and a brief description. Our team reviews all submissions.
How often is the park information updated?
Our team reviews and updates park data, entry fees, and facilities information quarterly. Wildlife and conservation status information is updated as new scientific data becomes available, typically twice a year.
Do you offer guided safari or tour bookings?
We are an information portal, not a tour operator. However, each park's detail page includes links to official park websites where you can find accredited tour operators, booking information, and permit requirements for your visit.
How can I support wildlife conservation?
There are many ways to help! Visiting national parks directly supports their funding. You can also donate to organisations like WWF, Wildlife Conservation Society, or local park foundations. Responsible eco-tourism and reducing your environmental footprint also make a significant impact.
Is the animal information scientifically accurate?
We source our wildlife data from reputable institutions including the IUCN Red List, WWF, National Geographic, and peer-reviewed wildlife research. Our team includes qualified wildlife biologists who review all animal information for accuracy.
How do I report incorrect information on the site?
We appreciate corrections! Please use the contact form on this page, select "Website Feedback" as your topic, and describe the issue including the specific page and section. Our content team will review and update the information promptly.

Rhincodon typus

The world's largest fish — a gentle giant reaching up to 12 meters in length. Despite their imposing size, whale sharks are filter feeders that subsist entirely on tiny plankton and small fish. They migrate thousands of kilometers annually.

🦅 Raptor — Bird
Bald Eagle
Haliaeetus leucocephalus

The national bird and symbol of the United States. Bald Eagles are powerful hunters and skilled fishers, diving feet-first to snatch fish from water. Once endangered due to DDT, their recovery is one of conservation's greatest success stories.

Rare & Endangered Species

Species on the brink — whose survival depends on protected park habitats.

⚠️ Endangered
Mountain Gorilla
Only ~1,000 remain in the wild. Found in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, Uganda and Virunga NP, Congo.
⚠️ Endangered
Bengal Tiger
Fewer than 2,500 survive in the wild. Protected primarily in India's Tiger Reserves and national parks.
⚠️ Vulnerable
Snow Leopard
Estimated 4,000–6,500 in the wild. Roams the high mountains of Central Asia in extreme isolation.
⚠️ Endangered
Whale Shark
Populations declining due to fishing and boat strikes. Protected in marine national parks worldwide.
